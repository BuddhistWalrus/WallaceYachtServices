Weekly & Monthly Maintenance

External wash; stain control; protective treatment to stainless and isinglass; complete internal cleaning; bilge and engine room inspection and operation of vessel to maintain seaworthiness.

Limited Vessel Operation

Includes any miscellaneous maneuvering or handling of vessel. To include sea trials, fueling, and delivering/returning vessel for service. Exercise program to be performed as requested.

Mechanical Repairs & Miscellaneous Services

Includes minor mechanical repairs and non-mechanical activities such as bilge and engine room detailing, purchasing of parts and supplies and overseeing of contractors.

Captain's Services

Includes trip preparation, operation of vessel and securing vessel upon return.

Bottom Cleaning

Performed monthly at the best rate available, while maintaining top quality work.

Compound & Wax Service

Performed quarterly at the best rate available. We wax our yachts every 3-6 months to prevent them from becoming oxidized or chalky.

About Us

Captain William S. Wallace ("Steve") is the Founder and President of Wallace Yacht Services, Inc. and holds a U.S. Coast Guard Master Marine 100 ton license.

The company was founded in 1986 to provide high quality marine services to boat owners in Southwest Florida, ensuring a sense of confidence and comfort. The company is based in Naples, Florida and strives to protect and maintain its clients' investments.
